State Farm Stadium

Located in Glendale, Arizona, just a short drive away from downtown Phoenix, State Farm Stadium delivers a world-class experience for fans. Home to the Arizona Cardinals, this state-of-the-art facility was the first to incorporate a retractable natural grass playing surface in North America and the first completely retractable roof operating at an incline. Its exterior skin of shimmering metal panels reflects the shifting desert light while 21 vertical glass-walled slots offer amazing views of the desert horizon. The stadium has previously hosted a pair of Super Bowls, the 2017 NCAA Final Four, and two College Football National Championships. Check out more events happening at State Farm Stadium.
